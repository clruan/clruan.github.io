Hello, I'm Conglin Ruan

Exploring the intersection of Biostatistics and Artificial Intelligence to solve complex biomedical problems.

I am a researcher passionate about leveraging data science to advance healthcare. My work focuses on developing and applying statistical and machine learning methods to analyze complex biomedical data.

I am currently applying for PhD programs in Biostatistics. My research interests lie at the convergence of:

  • Causal Inference
  • AI & ML
  • Spatial Transcriptomics
  • Biomedical Informatics
